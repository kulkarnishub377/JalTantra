The Problem We Solve

  • 54% of India faces water stress
  • 50% of irrigation water is wasted due to manual methods
  • Farmers lack access to crop disease tools and market analytics

Farming in India is critical—but farmers lack tools for precision, prediction, and profit.

Key Challenges Faced by Indian Farmers

💧

Water Wastage & Inefficient Irrigation

  • 30-50% irrigation water wasted
  • Depleting groundwater levels
  • Yield loss from over/underwatering
🌱

Crop Diseases Reduce Income

  • 30-40% crops lost yearly
  • Lack of expert guidance
  • High financial losses
🔬

Lack of Real-Time Crop Health Insights

  • Pest infestations ruin crops
  • Soil quality degrades
  • Uninformed decisions
🌦️

Unpredictable Climate & Poor Planning

  • Traditional farming fails in changing weather
  • No prediction tools for soil/weather
  • Lack of data-driven crop selection
🛠️

Expensive & Non-Durable Hardware

  • High cost of imported devices
  • Devices fail in Indian conditions
  • Limited servicing/spares
💸

Low Profits Due to Middlemen

  • Middlemen take up to 50% earnings
  • No direct buyer connection
  • Unfair low prices
📊

Lack of Market Insights

  • No real-time crop demand/pricing
  • Overproduction, financial loss
  • No access to competitive rates
🌐

Language Barriers in Tech Use

  • 90% struggle with English platforms
  • Most tools lack multi-language
  • Miss market trends & schemes
